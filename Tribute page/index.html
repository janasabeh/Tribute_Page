Fairuz

The Symphony of Lebanon

Timeline of the famous Singer:

  • 1933 -Born as Nuhad Haddad in Beirut,Lebanon
  • 1946 -Discovered By Mohammad Fleifel who was searching for a talent for his choral group
  • 1949 -First song "GHouroub", composed by Assi Rahbani and written by Moukarzel
  • 1952 -"Itab",first huge success in the Middle East
  • 1955 -Fairuz marries Assi Rahabani
  • 1959 -Performs in Baalbeck International Festival and Damascus International Ferstival
  • 1961 -Performances in Canada, USA, Brazil, and Argentina
  • 1964 -Fairuz sang "Give me the Flute and Sing" that establishes her as the Arab World's most prominent name in music.
  • 1965-1970 -Many of her plays are made into movies and famous songs.
  • 1973 -Ziad Rahbani, her eldest son, composes the first song for his mother titles "Saalouni Nnas"
  • 1981 -First conceert with her son Ziad, in Brazil. In US and Canada where ticket sales pass the 1 million mark.
  • 1980s-Present -At age of 87, Fairuz is the icon of lebanon.She still performs at many festivals and her latest song release was a remix of her famous song "Adaysh Kan Fe Nas".
